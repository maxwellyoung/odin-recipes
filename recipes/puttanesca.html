Puttanesca

Try it with a glass of Pinot Noir or Sirah.

This has been a very important meal for me. If you know me, you know I love to cook this pasta. It tastes like heaven. Spend a little. Try luxury.

Ingredients

  • chop chilli
  • clove of garlic; crushed
  • 4 olives
  • 1/2 tablespoon of capers
  • 100 g of tuna
  • cinnamon
  • 350g of passata
  • 15g of anchovy
  • 250g of pasta

Instructions

  1. oil into pan
  2. crush in unpeeled clove of garlic
  3. add capers, anchovies w their oil
  4. add finely chopped chilli
  5. cook for two minutes while stirring
  6. then add tuna, breaking up
  7. add olives
  8. stir in pinch of cinnamon
  9. add passata
  10. ** cook pasta fill and boil kettle
  11. pan & pot on low heat
  12. boiled water into the pot
  13. tablespoon of salt into pot
  14. then pasta
  15. pasta cooked drain , reserving some water
  16. tip pasta into sauce
  17. add olive oil & lemon juice
  18. toss
